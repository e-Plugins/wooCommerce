********************************************************************************
  Installation instructions for:
********************************************************************************

  Wordpress + Woocommerce plugin

********************************************************************************

-- Requirements ----------------------------------------------------------------

PHP 5.6+
Wordpress 4.9.0+
Woocommerce 3.4.0+

-- Installation ----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Install the latest version Wordpress

2. Install Woocommerce, go to your Wordpress Dashboard (admin login)
   and click on 'Plugins'. Then click 'Add new' and search for 'Woocommerce'.
   It will probably listed first as "WooCommerce - excelling eCommerce", click
   on 'Install now'.

3. Unzip the Digiwallet plugin. 

4. Drag the folder 'digiwallet' to the /wp-content/plugins
   of your Wordpress installation

5. Go back to 'Plugins' and click 'Activate' below 'Digiwallet for Woocommerce'

6. Now go to Woocommerce > Settings > Payment gateways

7. If you don't have one, you can get a free Digiwallet account on
   https://www.DigiWallet.com/

   Furthermore, there are not set-up costs, monthly fees, payout fees, etc.

8. Open the Tab 'digiwallet' and enter your outletid/layoutcode there.
   Also, tabs for 'iDEAL', 'Bancontact' and 'Sofort Banking' etc have been added.
   Open these too and activate the payment methods you like to include.

9. You're good to go!

   Note: you can switch the testmode on in your digiwallet enviroment.
   You can simulate real payments.
   Needless to say, but uncheck it again after testing, otherwise all your
   merchandise will be offered for free...
